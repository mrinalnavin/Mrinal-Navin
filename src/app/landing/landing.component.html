What we can use

In general, donations in the following categories represent the priority focus for us:

  • Pharmaceuticals
  • Over-the-counter (OTC) medicines
  • Medical and hospital supplies
  • Basic hygiene products
  • Infant formula
  • Vitamins & Nutritional supplements

All product offers will receive careful review prior to acceptance.

What we can't use

In general, the following product/industry areas are not accepted:

  • Expired Medicines
  • Complex medical equipment
  • Controlled-substances
  • Flammable products

We will provide for your medication if you can't afford it.
